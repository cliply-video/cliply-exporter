How it works

01

Paste a link

Any YouTube URL or video ID — downloaded locally with yt-dlp.

02

Import XML

SportsCode / Nacsport tags become colored clips. Optional — or just keep the video.

03

Export

MP4s in per-tag folders, plus reels. Stream-copy fast, or re-encode.
